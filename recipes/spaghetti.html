Spaghetti's Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish made with long, thin noodles served with a rich and flavorful tomato sauce. It is often prepared with ground meat, garlic, and herbs, creating a simple yet delicious meal. Spaghetti is a comforting and popular dish enjoyed by people of all ages around the world.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti pasta
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  5. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  6. Pour in the tomato sauce and stir well.
  7. Add oregano, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  8. Mix the cooked spaghetti with the sauce or serve the sauce on top.
  9. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
