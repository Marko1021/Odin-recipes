Lasagna's Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made with layers of flat pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It is baked in the oven until golden and bubbly. The combination of savory meat, tomato sauce, and melted cheese creates a warm and comforting meal that is loved around the world.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, then add a layer of noodles.
  7. Spread ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  9. Bake for 25–30 minutes until the cheese is melted and golden.
  10. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
