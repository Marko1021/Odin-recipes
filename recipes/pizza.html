Pizza's Recipe

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a soft dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of ingredients. It is baked in the oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Pizza is one of the most loved meals around the world and can be customized with different toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the dough on a baking tray or pizza pan.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Add pepperoni, mushrooms, and onions evenly over the cheese.
  7. Drizzle olive oil lightly over the toppings.
  8. Sprinkle oregano and a little salt on top.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  10. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot.
